Q1
Opening allowance for doubtful debts Rs.1000. Closing receivables Rs.100000. Using 5% allowance what amount should be charged to statement of comprehensive income?
A. Rs. 4000
B. Rs. 5000
C. Rs. 2000
D. Rs. 1000
Correct: A
Allow multiple: false (max 2)
Explanation: Required allowance = 100000 × 5% = 5000. Opening allowance = 1000. Charge to P&L = 5000 - 1000 = Rs.4000.
